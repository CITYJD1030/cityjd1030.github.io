訊號與系統

Signals and Systems

National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University
  • Dept. of course offering: College of Electrical Engineering
  • Required/Elective: Required
  • Instructor: 賴青沂

本課程為講授課程，並搭配作業練習與考試，涵蓋：

  • 線性時不變系統（LTI Systems）
  • 傅立葉級數與傅立葉轉換
  • 訊號與系統的時域與頻域特性
  • 取樣理論與失真分析
  • 拉氏轉換與 Z 轉換
  • 訊號處理與通訊系統中的應用案例

在這門課中，我系統性學習了連續與離散時間訊號與系統的數學建模與分析方法。課程以線性時不變系統（LTI）為核心，探討系統的穩定性、可解性與響應行為，並透過傅立葉級數與傅立葉轉換分析週期與非週期訊號的頻率組成。

課程內容亦涵蓋取樣理論、Aliasing 現象與還原條件，進一步延伸至拉氏轉換與 Z 轉換的應用，以處理連續與離散系統的輸入輸出關係與頻域行為。這些數學工具為後續訊號處理、通訊系統設計、控制系統分析與 AI 特徵擷取建立重要基礎。

Signals and Systems introduced the mathematical foundations of both continuous-time and discrete-time signal modeling. Through the study of LTI (Linear Time-Invariant) systems, I learned to analyze system stability, impulse response, and system behavior in both time and frequency domains using Fourier series and transforms.

The course also covered sampling theory, aliasing effects, and the use of Laplace and Z-transforms to solve differential/difference equations and analyze system responses. These tools are fundamental to advanced courses and applications such as digital signal processing (DSP), communications, control systems, and machine learning models involving frequency-domain representations.
